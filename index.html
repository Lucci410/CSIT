Mac & Cheese with Parmesan

The GREATEST Mac & Cheese ever

The Ingredients:

  1. 1 & 1/2 Pounds Macaroni
  2. Half pound of American cheese
  3. Half pound of Gouda cheese
  4. As much parmesan needed

The PROCESS:

  1. Combine both cheeses
  2. Combine Mac with Cheese
  3. Stir Mac & Cheese
  4. Cook for 20 minutes then add parmesan
  5. Cook for 5 minutes mix then add parmesan
  6. Cook for 20 minutes then done

The reason why this is my favorite Mac & Cheese is because growing up my grandmother use to make this for me all the time. This was her recipe that she would do ever since she came to America. Growing up my grandma was the person I looked up to. She taught me a lot of different things growing up and this recipe has stuck with me. As I know this is a common recipe this is my favorite one.
